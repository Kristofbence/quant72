Why We Built This

The problem: almost nobody who participates in them is using any of that research. They are buying 5¢ contracts on dramatic outcomes, executing with market orders, ignoring fees, and calling it trading. The data is brutal: 87.3% of Polymarket wallets are unprofitable. Taker-order traders lose 32% on average.

Beatpoly exists to close that gap. Everything we publish is grounded in peer-reviewed research or empirical platform data. Every strategy is testable, reproducible, and explained without jargon.

Our Principles

  • Data over narrative. Every claim is sourced. We cite the study, the sample size, and the methodology. If we cannot source it, we do not state it.
  • Process over outcome. A losing trade with positive EV is correct process. A winning trade with negative EV is incorrect process. We evaluate decisions by the math, not the result.
  • Survival first. No strategy works if you blow up. Kelly Criterion, fractional sizing, and category caps are not optional — they are the foundation everything else is built on.
  • Maker, not Taker. post_only: true is not a setting, it is a philosophy. We are liquidity providers, not liquidity consumers. We do not chase prices.
  • Free, always. The education is free because the business model is affiliate referrals and community — not selling you a course. The incentives are aligned: we only benefit when you actually start trading.

The Business Model (Transparency)

Beatpoly earns commission through affiliate partnerships with Kalshi and Polymarket. When you sign up through our links, we receive a small referral fee — funded by the platforms, not by you. The signup bonuses you receive are also funded by the platforms.
