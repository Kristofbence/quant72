Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Beatpoly ("we", "us", "our") operates the website beatpoly.com. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal data when you use our site and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information and only what is necessary to operate the service:

  • Email address and name — when you voluntarily submit the community signup or notification forms on our site. This is submitted to Web3Forms and stored in our email list for the purpose of sending you updates about new content and community access.
  • Usage data — standard web analytics (page views, referrer, browser type) collected anonymously. We do not currently use Google Analytics but may add it in the future, at which point this policy will be updated.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you educational content updates, new article notifications, and community access information
  • To improve the site and understand which content is most useful
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes

Affiliate Links

Beatpoly contains affiliate links to Kalshi and Polymarket. When you click these links and create an account, we may receive a commission from the platform. This commission does not affect the price you pay or the bonuses you receive. Affiliate activity is tracked by the platforms using referral codes embedded in the URLs — we do not have access to your account data on those platforms.

Cookies

Beatpoly does not use tracking cookies. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Standard browser session data is handled by your browser and not stored by us.

Third-Party Services

  • Web3Forms — handles form submissions. Your name and email are transmitted to Web3Forms' servers when you submit a form. See their privacy policy at web3forms.com.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log font requests. See Google's privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages — the site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log request metadata per their privacy policy.

Data Retention and Deletion

If you submitted your email and wish to be removed from our list, email us at the address below and we will delete your information within 7 business days.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR for EU residents and CCPA for California residents), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us directly.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us via the community form on our homepage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
